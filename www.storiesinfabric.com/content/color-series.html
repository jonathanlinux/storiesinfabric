Color Series

At one point in my career I spent my days in administrative work. After five years I reentered my studio and was stymied. It seemed as if I had lost the self-discipline to free my mind of daily living and allow myself to create. A colleague suggested that rather than pummel my psyche as I was laboriously churning out one uninspired piece after another that I simply put pieces I wasnâ€™t connecting with away and begin again. Thus my geometric and color series was launched. Simple shapes - circles and squares and such of various sizes - are paired with color to express a concept: Fall, Winter, Summer, Spring, Firelight, Tough Sledding, Moonlit beach, Exuberance.
