Commissions

My work is included in the collections of the National Institutes of Health, Kaiser Permanente, The Children's Inn, The Greater Hospital of Baltimore, G Street Fabrics, the State Department’s Art in Embassies program, and other private and corporate collections.

Recently I completed a commission for an assisted living home in the D. C. area. Two of the pieces are in the main room. One is above the reception desk and the other above the fireplace. The other four are “way finders.” Each floor has a theme. Posters and pictures follow the theme of Washington D. C., New York, London, and entertainment. My pieces are also theme related. They are hung opposite the main elevators on each floor. My entertainment piece portrays images of vintage movie stars generously lent for the purpose by photographer Murray Garrett. I thank him for his kindness. Because of his images my collage comes alive and provides much enjoyment for those who live in the home.
