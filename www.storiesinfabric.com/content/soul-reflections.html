Soul Reflections

Diversity, Peace, Historical Tumult are subjects that inspire and haunt me. I am reminded of the adage “If we don’t speak, who will?” and know that I must contribute my part if only in a small way. The comforting texture of fabric united with difficult subject matter reminds me of fiber’s resilient properties. Fabric is pliant as are personalities. If we each stop to think before we speak and do, the world can change - one person at a time. I would like these pieces to provide a stimulus for thought and reflection.
